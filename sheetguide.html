How to use the response sheet

Hi, now that you have access to the response sheet, just some notes before you get cracking.

  1. Once you have access to the sheet, please add your details to the second sheet 'Researchers and MPs' if it's not the list already.
  2. If you would like to file a requested question, mark your name in Column H; once you do, it is your responsibility to complete the other steps:
    1. Contact the person who requested the question be filed if you need more help/research regarding the subject, or simply acknowledging that you'll be filing it with(/out) modifications. [Column I]
    2. Contact the person again when you've filed it. [Column J]
    3. Contact the person if the question wasn't selected. [Column K]
    4. Finally, share the Ministry's response when it's available. [Column L]
We're maintaining this process to ensure accountability on your part, and so that the researchers requesting these questions feel valued and are encouraged to contribute more questions in the future.

For help, contact parliamentques [at] gmail [dot] com
